Overview

PPInsights does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services of any kind.

This policy describes what data the app handles, how it handles it, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect
We collect no data of any kind from users of this application.

No personal information, usage statistics, device identifiers, or behavioral data are gathered, logged, or transmitted to us at any time.

Third-Party Services

PPInsights connects to the Proofpoint Reporting API to retrieve security data on your behalf. This connection is made directly between the app and Proofpoint's servers using credentials you supply. We do not intermediate, log, or retain any data returned by the API.

Your use of the Proofpoint Reporting API is governed by Proofpoint's own privacy policy:

https://www.proofpoint.com/us/privacy-policy

Credentials & Local Storage

Any API credentials you enter in the app are stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain — Apple's secure credential storage system. Credentials are never transmitted to us or any third party other than Proofpoint directly, and only for the purpose of fulfilling your API requests.
